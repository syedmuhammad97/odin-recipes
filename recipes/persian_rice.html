Persian Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 quarts water
  • 3 tablespoon salt
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 3 tablespoon butter
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 potato, peeled and sliced
  • 1 pinch ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Bring water and salt to a boil in a large pot. Add rice and stir. Boil for 7 minutes. Drain rice and rinse with cold water.
  2. Heat butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add saffron and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add rice and stir to coat. Reduce heat to low and cook, covered, until rice is tender, about 30 minutes.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add potato slices and cook until golden brown, about 5 minutes. Remove from skillet and drain on paper towels.
  4. Season rice with cumin and stir in parsley. Top with potato slices.

Enjoy your Persian Rice!
